What is Data science ?

Data science can be defined simply as the method by which you can make better commercial and professional decisions. It uses scientific methods and mathematics to process data and extracts meaningful information from it. The process involves using powerful hardware along with an efficient algorithm and software programs to process the available data in order to obtain valuable knowledge and solve the data problems.

Role of the Data Scientist :

A data scientist may need to wear different hats depending on their job description. The job of a data scientist is to do more than just analyze the data and share the results obtained. He/she is also responsible for creating the machine learning algorithms used in the data analysis. The scientist is also expected to manage the software platforms and create data products. Most importantly, data scientists are expected to use their background knowledge and knowledge of the data analysis tools to identify solutions, optimize processes, and improve company performance.
